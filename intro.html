Aeternum

The Chalice of

Eternity

Centuries ago, in a time long forgotten, the Chalice of Eternity was crafted by a revered sorcerer named Elyon the Wise. Elyon was known for his unparalleled mastery of magic and his quest for the ultimate source of wisdom. The chalice was forged from celestial metals and imbued with ancient runes, each representing the fundamental forces of the universe.

As the centuries passed.....

the Chalice of Eternity disappeared from recorded history.
